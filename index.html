About

Viktor's coaching philosophy is built on discipline, technique, respect, and confidence. Originally from Russia, where he spent years developing elite fighters, Viktor relocated to the UK to continue his mission: helping athletes of all ages unlock their potential through martial arts.

Whether working with children learning their first techniques, teenagers preparing for competition, or adults seeking fitness and self-defense skills, Viktor tailors every training program to the individual. His approach combines technical precision with mental resilience, ensuring students grow both on and off the mat.
